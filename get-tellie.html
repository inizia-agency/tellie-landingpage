What Tellie is

Tellie is a warm conversational companion that offers friendly daily talks, emotional support, and a familiar voice to turn to whenever someone needs connection.

Why it matters

Later-life loneliness can quietly affect confidence, wellbeing, and quality of life. When family cannot always be there, the silence can feel heavier than it looks.
