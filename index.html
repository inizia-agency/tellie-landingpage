Feel Connected. Every Day.

Tellie is your warm, friendly AI companion, ready to listen, chat, and make every day brighter.

The problem

Loneliness in later life is more than a quiet house. It can slowly affect health, confidence, and joy. Public-health research shows that lacking social connection is linked to earlier death, and around 1 in 4 older adults experience social isolation.

Why Tellie?

Tellie gives your parent a warm, familiar voice to talk to every day, creating small moments of comfort, conversation, and connection when family cannot always be there.

About Us

Why We Exist

We started Tellie because we saw too many older adults spending days without a single meaningful conversation. We believe no one deserves to feel invisible.

Our Mission

To create technology that feels human, warm, understanding, and genuinely caring. Our mission is to make every person feel heard, valued, and connected.

What We Believe

We believe in dignity, kindness, and the simple power of a conversation. No matter your age, your stories matter.

Our Promise

We promise to be here every day to help you feel less alone. Because connection isn’t just important, it’s everything.
