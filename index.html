About Us

Why We Exist

We started Tellie because we saw too many older adults spending days without a single meaningful conversation. We believe no one deserves to feel invisible.

Our Mission

To create technology that feels human, warm, understanding, and genuinely caring. Our mission is to make every person feel heard, valued, and connected.

What We Believe

We believe in dignity, kindness, and the simple power of a conversation. No matter your age, your stories matter.

Our Promise

We promise to be here every day to help you feel less alone. Because connection isn’t just important, it’s everything.
